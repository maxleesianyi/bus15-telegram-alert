# v0.6.0 - Earlier alerts and Telegram bus lookups

## What changed

- Weekday Bus 15 alerts now run at 7:45, 8:00, 8:15, and 8:30 AM Singapore time.
- You can send the Telegram bot a message in the format `Bus 20 76953` to receive the next and subsequent arrivals for that service at that stop.
- `stop`, `pause`, `done`, `resume`, and `start` continue to control only the scheduled Bus 15 alerts for the current day.

## Reliability

- The live schedule remains QStash calling the Vercel endpoint with authenticated retries.
- The old 9 AM QStash schedule is removed automatically when the schedule setup script is run.

## Testing

- Automated Node tests cover scheduled message wording and the new Telegram lookup parser.
